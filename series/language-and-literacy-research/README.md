# Sutton SignWriting Language and Literacy Research

Repository and series-about page for the `Sutton SignWriting Language and Literacy Research` series.

## What This Series Is

`Sutton SignWriting Language and Literacy Research` is the research-facing lane of the Sutton SignWriting platform.

It carries the more disciplined developmental, literacy, and reading questions that should not be forced into the public `Sutton SignWriting Foundations` lane.

It now also carries the practical research-program layer for study design, materials, corpora, and reusable research infrastructure.

This series is for:

- psycholinguists
- cognitive scientists
- literacy researchers
- language researchers
- sign-language researchers with developmental or experimental interests
