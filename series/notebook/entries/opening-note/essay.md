# Opening Note

**Why this notebook exists and what belongs here**

This entry opens the `Notebook` series.

The notebook exists so that useful material does not have to choose too early among:

- `Sutton SignWriting Foundations`
- `Sutton SignWriting Language and Literacy Research`
- `Writing and Representation in Sutton SignWriting`

Some ideas are:

- valuable
- generative
- worth preserving
- not yet settled enough for the stronger public lanes

This series is where that work can live honestly.

## What belongs here

Likely notebook material includes:

- pathway sketches not yet ready for a research paper
- reflections on sound, visuality, and symbolic extension
- early viewpoint and perceptual ideas
- planning-adjacent conceptual notes
- drafts that may later graduate into stronger series

## What does not belong here

This is not:

- a dumping ground
- a substitute for making real series decisions
- the main public route into the platform

If an entry clearly belongs in a stronger series, it should move there.

## Current status

This opening note is intentionally simple.

Its job is to make the series real and to establish its boundaries before more exploratory entries appear.
