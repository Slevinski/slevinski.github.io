# X Threads Plan

**The Science of Sutton SignWriting**  
16-week expanded publishing run for X

## Series goal

This plan turns the current science series into a sustained X publishing program.

The purpose is to:

- define the terrain
- extend the conceptual frame
- introduce the two current presentations in stages
- bring different audiences into the grant and collaboration conversation over time

## Cadence

Recommended weekly posting schedule in Central Time:

- Tuesday, 11:00 a.m. - main thesis
- Wednesday, 1:00 p.m. - evidence or example
- Thursday, 11:00 a.m. - implication for science, literacy, or infrastructure
- Friday, 10:30 a.m. - collaboration ask, audience bridge, or link-forward close

Recommended audience levels:

- Deaf community and SignWriting users
