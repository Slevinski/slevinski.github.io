# University of Iowa

Repository and series-about page for the `University of Iowa` series.

## What This Series Is

`University of Iowa` is an institutional collaboration series for Iowa-facing outreach, PI or lab base, and practical research-support conversations.

It is tailored to University of Iowa faculty, grants staff, development staff, and prospective academic collaborators.

It is not the general Sutton SignWriting infrastructure series, though it can connect readers to the broader platform when needed.

The generalized infrastructure, philanthropy, host-structure, and precedent materials live in `Sutton SignWriting Infrastructure and Program`.

## Public Reading Path

Public readers usually need one of four things:

- a quick internal summary of the Iowa opportunity
- a PI-facing case for a PI or lab base
- a practical value case for active research teams
- a path into the longer Sutton SignWriting infrastructure conversation

The public overview lives as a series entry:

- [entries/overview/essay.md](./entries/overview/essay.md)

## Series Contents

- `entries/overview/` - public overview for the Iowa-facing collaboration case
- `entries/research-team-value/` - practical research value case for active teams
- `entries/pi-or-lab-base/` - PI-facing case for an institutional base
- `entries/outreach-memo/` - short internal memo for first conversations

## Publication And Citation Model

The likely publication model is:

- overview: web-first series overview
- outreach and institutional-base materials: bridge documents, not DOI-first
- broader infrastructure references: cite or connect to `Sutton SignWriting Infrastructure and Program`

This series should remain institution-specific and should not be forced into the eventual public `sutton-signwriting.io` core.
