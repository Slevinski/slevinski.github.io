# Operational Model, Staffing, and Budget Expansion

_Companion addendum for **Sutton SignWriting Infrastructure Core and Philanthropy**_

The operational model behind the proposed Infrastructure Core includes staffing, global coordination, infrastructure needs, and the conceptual budget shape.

## Core Staffing Model

The Infrastructure Core would include:

### Leadership

* Steve Slevinski as technical lead and steward
* Lead Principal Investigator

### Core Team

* Infrastructure Assistant for long-term continuity and succession
* Lab Manager for operations, coordination, and logistics
* Research Assistant for data, documentation, and project support

### Extended Contributors

* Adam Frost in a stipend-supported collaboration role
* Country Ambassadors, initially volunteer and later stipend-supported

## Ambassador Model

The ambassador model creates:

* local institutional access
* cultural and linguistic adaptation
* international relationship capacity

Initial countries:

* Brazil
* Mexico
* South Korea

Ambassadors support:

* outreach
* partnerships
* documentation of use
* grant and donor support

## Infrastructure Requirements

### Technical

* cloud hosting and domain stewardship
* storage for active and archival materials
* research Git and repository infrastructure
* Globus for data transfer
* sites and web infrastructure

### Hardware

* workstations
* storage systems
* backup systems

## Programmatic Expansion

### Annual SignWriting Symposium

A funded annual event could provide:

* 3 days to 1 week of programming
* hybrid participation with in-person and online access
* global participation
* research, education, and community programming

It would support:

* visibility
* collaboration
* legitimacy
* network growth

## Budget Shape

### Core Annualized Estimate

* personnel for the core team as the largest budget component
* infrastructure and hosting
* stipends for contributors and ambassadors
* symposium event support

### Budget Philosophy

* start small and durable
* expand with success
* support continuity first
* scale through grants and partnerships
